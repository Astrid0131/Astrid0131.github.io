About

Welcome to the first International Symposium on Building Performance and Diagnostics, exclusively featuring graduates from Carnegie Mellon University's Building Performance and Diagnostics program. This event highlights the achievements and innovations of CMU BPD alumni as they explore the impact of building performance on people and cities. Throughout the symposium, graduates will share insights on improving building design/operation/control and urban planning. Topics will cover areas from enhancing individual well-being to optimizing how cities function.
